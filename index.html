Computer Science

Computer Science is one of if not the fastest-rising majors, with tons of opportunities for high-paying employment. But what exactly will you do if you decide to pursue a career in computer science? This website is designed to be a quick guide that will introduce you to the field and some of the core classes in the Clemson Computer Science curriculum and briefly describe some helpful resources available to you as a student.

Popular Fields

Data Science

The study of Data Science has exploded within the last decade and continues to grow rapidly. Data Science is the study of interpreting data using various techniques to generate meaningful statistics and other information from a large quantity of data.

Learn More

Software Engineering

Software engineering is the intensive development of software using various techniques to develop, maintain, and test computer software. When you think of Computer Science, you will likely think of a Software Engineer because it is one of the most popular Career Paths to pursue with a Computer Science degree.

Learn More

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is the programming of computer systems, mainly using machine learning, to perform tasks on our behalf, much like a human would. AI is an exploding industry, with the recent ChatGPT showing how powerful AI can be.
