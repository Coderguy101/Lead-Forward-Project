Helpful Resources

Starting a degree path in Computer Science can be very challenging, and many new and current students need to be aware of what resources are out there to help them. This page will briefly introduce you to some key resources that will aid you in pursuing a degree in Computer Science. Many other helpful resources will not be listed here, but these three resources listed below are a great place to start.

Scroll Down To Get Started

Rate My Professor

Rate my professor allows you to see what previous students think of a professor

Why Rate My Professor Is Helpful

  • You can gage what the majority of students think about a professor
  • You can generally gage how difficult a professor will make their class
  • You can see what students say about the teacher's teaching methods, structure of the class, etc
  • You can gain valuable insight on a particular class
  • You can see how long a certain teacher has taught for by checking the review count
  • WARNING: Be careful the review is about the professor, not the class. There are many hated classes with a good teacher who gets uncessesary hate because of the nature of the class
  • WARNING: People are generally more likely to write bad reviews than good ones so to avoid skewed data, make sure the professor has a lot of reviews before judging.

Tiger Grader

Tiger Grader Allows you to view the past grade distribution of any class at Clemson

Why Tiger Grader is Helpful

  • It allows you to see the grade distribution of a certain class with respect to the teacher
  • It can help you plan your schedule by helping you decide which courses are generally going to be more difficult, which will help you build a balanced schedule.
  • Can be used along with RateMyProfessor to choose top professors with good grade distribtuion
  • Will help you see which classes are going to likely be weed out courses
  • TIP: When choosing Gen-Ed classes, make sure the A-grade distribution is at least 40%
  • TIP: If you are having trouble deciding if you should take a class with a great teacher or an easy one, it is generally better to go with the better teacher because you'll learn more

Clemson's Career Center

Why Clemson's Career Center is Excellent

  • Clemson's Career Center is one of the top ranked Career Centers in the Country providing many helpful services
  • Clemson's Co-Op education allows students to spend a semester working for a company which can later lead to full time employment
  • Clemson's JobLink allows students to find jobs specificly targeted for them
  • Clemson's Career Center hosts tons of networking events to help you get to know potential employers
  • Clemson's Career Center hosts a Career fair 2 times a year allowing students to meet with employers
  • Clemson's Career Center has an internship program that helps pair students with companies
